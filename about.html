About us

We’re like a house party at your friends’ friends: we’re relaxed, effortless and fun. Designed by a London/Seoul team, our pieces are a grown-up interpretation of the preppy streetwear worn by those cool Korean kids, with a touch of London edge.

Side Party is a ‘pieces brand’: we design key pieces that are missing from your wardrobe, giving them our own, fresh twist.

We're launching in 2015 - East London, Seoul and online.
